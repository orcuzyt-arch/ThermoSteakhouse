Thermo Steakhouse was born from a singular obsession — the pursuit of the perfect steak. In the heart of Al Khobar, we bring live-fire mastery, prime-grade sourcing, and an atmosphere that turns every dinner into an occasion.

From hand-selected Wagyu to show-stopping Tomahawks, every plate is a testament to craft, heat, and passion.
